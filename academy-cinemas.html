About

Welcome to the Academy Cinemas! Here you're family. Located in beautiful downtown portland, we show a variety of movies. We were founded in 1955 by Sir. Francis McMan. Sir. McMan wanted a place to show his independent movie collection in Portland. His buisness grew fast and unexpectedly. Francis was suddenly running one of the largest movie theatres in Portland. McMan hired more and more empolyees to deal with the growing demand, and ended up adding 3 more auditoriums to the theatre in 1960. McMan passed away in 1990 at the age of 85 from a heart attack in the projection room and left the Academy to his son. Today the Academey still keeps special showing times reserved for Sir. McMan's orginal movie collection. It's even said that people sometimes hear a man laughing at Sir. McMan's favorite comedy "Darling Of Mine" when the auditorium is empty.
